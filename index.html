Apps for the Little Ones

Tapappapa: Number Racing!


Tapappapa creates fun little worlds. Toddlers learn by playing and they learn a lot between ages 1 and 3. During these important years, toddlers will enjoy playing simple games with their parents and other caregivers.


This app contains ten "racing tracks". It's main purpose is to tap on the driving car and score points.


This app is safe. No banners, no in-app purchases, no links.
